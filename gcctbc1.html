GCC-TBC (Government Certificate In Computer Typing Basic Course) exam is conducted by MSCE Pune. This course is required in central government recruitment and it is mandatory for Group 'C' and Group 'D' posts in Maharashtra and Goa states.


Objective :

  • To merge Typing and latest Computer course so that student can work in highly competitive world.
  • To design the course and exam in such way that student will get deep understanding on typing and latest computer software’s and technology which has been using in government and private organization.

Eligibility Criteria :

  • 8th Standard Passed from any recognized board in india.
  • 30 wpm speed exam is prerequisite to appear for 40 wpm speed exam. or
  • Register to both 30 wpm and 40 wpm speed subject during the same term/ exam.

Course Details :

Course GCC-TBC
( Government Certificate in Computer Typing Basic Course)
Duration 6 months
Subjects  English, Marathi, Hindi
Speed 30 wpm, 40 wpm

Exam : Exam is conducted Twice in a year by MSCE PUNE. Visit MSCE PUNE
