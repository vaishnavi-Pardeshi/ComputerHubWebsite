Who We Are

Lande Computer Education Hub is a Government recognized computer typing institute and computer learning center in Amravati district, Maharashtra. We provide courses to learn Computer Typing, basics in computer and skills related to computer including: GCC-TBC (Govt. computer typing), Tally, Stenography. We also provide banking services, cyber cafe for online application. We follow a systematic way to make learning easy. Lande Computer Education Hub is one of the Best Computer institute in Amravati (Maharashtra ).
